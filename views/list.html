Health Food

  1. Water
    • Drink 8 to 12 cups of water daily.
  2. Dark Green Vegetables
    • Eat at least three to four times a week
    • Good options include broccoli, peppers, brussel sprouts and leafy greens like kale and spinach.
  3. Whole Grains
    • Eat at least two or three times daily
    • whole wheat flour, rye, oatmeal, barley, amaranth, quinoa or a multigrain
  4. Beans and Lentils
    • Eat a bean-based meal at least once a week
    • Try to add legumes, including beans and lentils, to soups, stews, casseroles, salads and dips or eat them plain
  5. Fish
    • Eat two to three serving of fish a week
    • A serving consists of 3 to 4 ounces of cooked fish
    • Good choices are salmon, trout, herring, bluefish, sardines and tuna.
  6. Berries
    • Include two to four servings of fruit in your diet each day
    • eat berries such as raspberries, blueberries, blackberries and strawberries.
